About The Buoy

The Buoy is Jacksonville’s newest coastal-inspired eatery, drawing from the St. Johns River and Atlantic vibes that define our city.

We focus on fresh, local seafood—grilled shrimp, oysters on the half shell, fish tacos, Lowcountry boils, and chef’s daily catches—served alongside craft cocktails, local beers, and a phenomenal wine list. The atmosphere is relaxed and nautical, blending old Florida charm with a modern touch.

Whether you're fresh off the water, gathering with friends, or just in the mood for great coastal flavors, The Buoy is your go-to spot for quality food, friendly service, and good times.
